# Bakery FAQ

Q: What are your opening hours?
A: We are open Monday to Friday from 7 AM to 7 PM, and weekends from 8 AM to 6 PM, off on Mondays

Q: Do you offer gluten-free options?
A: Yes, we have a variety of gluten-free breads and pastries available daily.

Q: Can I place custom cake orders?
A: Yes, custom cake orders require 48 hours advance notice. Please contact us directly for special requests. 

Q: Do you do carrot cake?
